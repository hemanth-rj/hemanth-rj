### What I work on

I research music recommender systems: specifically how they fail new users, underserve longtail artists, and trade off diversity against engagement.

I collaborated with CORRD, a music discovery platform, on their music recommendation pipeline, working on embedding-based search, description quality evaluation, and A/B testing framework design.

### Before research

### What I'm building toward

Personalization product management in music streaming - the intersection of recommendation systems research, user persona and engineering.
